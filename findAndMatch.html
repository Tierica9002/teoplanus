Game - "Find & Match"

Welcome!

Click "Start" to begin the game of all time...

Instructions:

Goal is to clear all the boxes on the board by matching the given artist name name with the hidden name in each box.
You have 3 lives in total and 3 seconds for each (each time the countdown reaches 0, 1 life is depleted).

The game continues once you have found all the names. Part II is about dragging an artist's portrait to his / her corresponding instrument.
